About SPÉ…VE

Spave is a student-made app with the goal of helping
others to save money with convenience.

Less is more.

Spave's minimalistic and sleek design helps you easily
access the tools you need, minus the chaos.

The math is on us!

Taxes...tips...discounts... no one wants to deal with
all those numbers. We developed selective built-in
calculators to do the math for you. With a few
simple clicks, you get results within seconds.

All in one place.

We even embedded other features: a webpage with
the latest deals, a notes page, and a fun facts section
to keep you entertained. Why hassle between apps,
when you can have it all in one place?
